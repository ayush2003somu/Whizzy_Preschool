About Us

Whizzy Preschool is a nurturing learning space dedicated to early childhood education. Our goal is to create a joyful and safe environment where young learners can develop confidence, creativity, and curiosity.

We believe that the early years of a child’s life are the most important for building a strong foundation for future learning. Through engaging activities, play-based learning, and caring guidance from trained teachers, we help children grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally.
Our classrooms are colourful, interactive, and carefully designed to support the development of every child.

Our Vision / Mission

Our Mission
At Whizzy Preschool, our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where young children can begin their educational journey with joy and confidence. We aim to deliver high-quality early childhood education that focuses on building strong foundations in learning, discipline, creativity, and moral values.


Our Vision Our vision is to become a trusted and respected preschool that shapes the future of young learners by providing a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We strive to inspire children to become confident, creative, and compassionate individuals who are prepared for the challenges of the future.

Traditional Values

We believe that education should nurture both knowledge and values. Our preschool focuses on the following principles:

  • A safe and positive value-based environment
  • Education focused on discipline and moral values
  • Building confidence and responsibility in children
  • Celebration of festivals and cultural events
  • Focus on overall child development
  • Learning through play, art, and activities
  • Respect for Indian culture and traditions
  • Yoga, meditation, and healthy living

Why Choose Us

Whizzy Preschool is a nurturing learning space dedicated to early childhood education.

  • Eco-friendly atmosphere
