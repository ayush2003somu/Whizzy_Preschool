Academic Programs

Curriculums designed to foster cognitive, emotional, and social development.

  • Play Group – Early learning through play and exploration
  • Junior KG – Introduction to basic concepts and creativity
  • Senior KG – Preparation for primary education
  • Balvatika – Development of language, numbers, and social skills

Educational Facilities

At Whizzy Preschool, we provide a nurturing and engaging environment where young learners can explore, grow, and build a strong educational foundation. Our programs focus on both academic and personal development through a variety of learning methods and activities.

  • Play-Based Learning Approach – Children learn through interactive play, storytelling, music, art, and creative activities that make learning fun.
  • Abacus Learning – Special focus on number recognition and logical thinking through Abacus techniques.
  • Activity-Based Teaching – Lessons are designed with hands-on activities to help children understand concepts easily.
  • Language Development – Focus on communication skills, vocabulary building, and confident speaking.
  • Creative Arts and Craft – Drawing, coloring, and craft activities encourage imagination and creativity.
  • Yoga and Meditation – Simple yoga exercises and meditation help improve concentration and healthy habits.
  • Cultural and Festival Celebrations – Children celebrate Indian festivals and cultural events to learn traditions and values.
  • Moral and Value-Based Education – Teaching discipline, respect, kindness, and responsibility.
  • Individual Attention – Small class sizes allow teachers to focus on every child’s development.
  • Preparation for Primary School – Structured learning in Senior KG and Balvatika prepares children for primary education.

Providing a safe, nurturing, and beautiful environment for your child to learn, play, and grow. Our mission is to create joyful learning experiences that help children build confidence and lifelong curiosity.
